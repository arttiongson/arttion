Privacy Policy

Jarvis Personal Assistant · Last updated May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Jarvis Personal Assistant SMS service ("Jarvis", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information. Jarvis is a private, non-commercial hobbyist project operated by an individual for personal use.

Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to operate the service:

  • Your mobile phone number.
  • The content of SMS messages you send to and receive from the service.

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to deliver personal assistant notifications and to respond to messages you send. We do not use your information for advertising or marketing of any kind.

Sharing of information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes. No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third party.

We use trusted service providers solely to operate the service — for example, our SMS provider (Twilio) to transmit messages on our behalf. These providers may process your information only as needed to deliver the service and are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

Data retention

Messages may be retained only as long as needed to operate the assistant. You may request deletion of your information at any time using the contact details below.

Opt-out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. See our SMS Terms & Conditions for details.
